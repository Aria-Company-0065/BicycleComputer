PRIVACY POLICY

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
1.1 Recorded Data
The this application uses the GPS in your device to record location data by the foreground service. The data recorded includes latitude & longitude, speed, distance travelled, elevation, and time. This data is recorded even when the app is in the background, such as while using another app, making a phone call, or turning off the device screen. We store the recorded data only on your device and we do not share it with third parties. The recorded data does not leave your device unless you choose to export it.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected
We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. No information is collected that can identify you as an individual.
